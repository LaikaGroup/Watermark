Refund Process for iOS

If you would like to request a refund for your purchase of Watermark Copyright on iOS, please follow the steps below:

  1. Open the App Store on your iOS device.
  2. Tap on your profile picture in the top right corner.
  3. Tap on "Purchased" to view your list of purchased apps.
  4. Find Watermark Copyright in the list and tap on it.
  5. Tap on "Report a Problem" and select the appropriate option.
  6. Follow the on-screen instructions to submit your refund request.

Please note that refund requests are subject to the App Store's refund policy and approval.
